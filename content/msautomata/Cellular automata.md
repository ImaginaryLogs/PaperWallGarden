A **cellular automaton** is a [model](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_modeling "Scientific modeling") used in [computer science](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_science "Computer science") and [mathematics](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematics "Mathematics"). The idea is to model a [dynamic system](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dynamic_system?action=edit&redlink=1 "Dynamic system (not yet started)") by using a number of [cell](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cell "Cell")s. Each cell has one of many possible states. With each "turn" or [iteration](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iteration "Iteration") the state of the current cell is determined by two things: its current state, and the states of the neighbouring cells.
