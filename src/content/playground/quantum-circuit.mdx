# Quantum Circuit Simulator

This playground lets you build quantum circuits gate-by-gate and observe
the resulting qubit state live on the [Bloch sphere](/garden/quantum/bloch-sphere-notes).

> See also: [[bloch-sphere-notes]] in the Digital Garden for the mathematical
> derivation of Bloch sphere coordinates.

<WasmLoader modulePath="/wasm/quantum-sim/quantum_sim.js">
  {(api, ready) => (
    ready ? (
      <div className="circuit-playground">
        <CircuitEditor quantumApi={api} />
        <BlochSphere state={api.getState()} />
      </div>
    ) : (
      <div className="loading-wasm">⚙️ Compiling quantum simulation engine...</div>
    )
  )}
</WasmLoader>
