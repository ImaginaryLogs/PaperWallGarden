The **Hamiltonian** of a system is an [operator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operator_\(physics\) "Operator (physics)") corresponding to the total energy of that system, including both [kinetic energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinetic_energy "Kinetic energy") and [potential energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Potential_energy "Potential energy").

Because it can be usually encoded in a matrix, the [[Solving Eigenstates]] of Hamiltonians makes its possible to measure possible outcomes as a measurement of the system's total energy through its [[Spectrum]].

Making future predictions is possible through solving a Hamiltonian of System.
